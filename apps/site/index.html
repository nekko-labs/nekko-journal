What's inside

Everything to remember your year — nothing to feel guilty about

The complete app is free. Premium only adds reach and safety, never a paywall on writing.

📝

A monthly journal

One markdown entry a month, with a natural editor and a formatting toolbar. Write as much or as little as you like.

🎯

Goals, placed in time

Drag each yearly goal onto the month where it'll happen. Watch progress add up across the year, never as a fragile streak.

📸

Photos & moments

Attach captioned photos to the goals you're working on. A full-screen lightbox to relive them later.

🔭

Look back

A timeline, "this month last year," and a year-in-review. The whole point: see what you actually did.

✨

Journaling assist

Reflection prompts, month summaries, and a year-in-review draft. Works offline; bring your own Claude key to go further.

🔒

Your data, your files

Runs fully offline. Open a real folder on disk and your journal is written as plain files you own. Export any time.

The ritual

Three calm steps, once a month

Set the year

Add your goals and a word for the year. Drag each goal onto the month you'll tackle it.

Live the month

When the month ends, write what happened. Add photos, mark goals done, note how it felt.

Look back

Scroll your timeline, revisit this month last year, and let a year-in-review show you the shape of it.
