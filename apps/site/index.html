THE MONTHLY RITUAL

01

Set your goals for the year

A short list of things that matter. Not resolutions, not habits to track: intentions.

02

Place each one in time

Drag every goal onto the month where it belongs. The year stops being a blur and becomes a shape.

03

Write once a month

One quiet entry with the moments that mattered, a mood, and a few photos. Then close the app and live.

Daily is a chore. A year is a blur.

A month is just right: often enough to remember, rare enough to stay calm. You show up about once a month, and that's the whole point.

Progress, not streaks

Goals add up across the year instead of living or dying by a fragile chain. Miss a month and nothing breaks.

Yours, on your disk

Runs fully offline. Open a folder you own and your journal is written as plain files. Export any time. Sync only if you want it.

Free is the whole app.

Every surface, unlimited goals and entries, local photos, and export cost nothing. Premium only adds cross-device sync, more photos, and cloud backup.
